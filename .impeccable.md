# Design Context

## Target Audience

People using a casual decision app when they are uncertain, stuck between options, or want a quick outside nudge. The app should feel personal enough for relationship, money, work, and life-choice questions, but not serious enough to look like therapy, finance, or productivity software.

## Use Cases

- Type a question and shake the 8-ball to get a contextual answer.
- Dictate a question with voice input, confirm it, then shake the 8-ball.
- Use cursor, touch, or phone motion to make the reveal feel physical.
- Ask another question quickly after an answer.

## Brand Personality And Tone

Quiet, decisive, tactile. The interface should be modern and minimal, with the massive 8-ball as the memorable object. It should feel like a restrained oracle toy: precise controls around a glossy physical centerpiece, not a generic AI chat UI.

## Quality Bar

Finished MVP, not throwaway prototype. The app should run locally, have tested core logic, and feel visually polished enough to share.
